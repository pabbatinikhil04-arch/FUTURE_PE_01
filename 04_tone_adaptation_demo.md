# Output 04 — Tone Adaptation Demonstration
**Purpose:** Prove the prompt framework is reusable across different local business categories, not just rewritten once for this client.

Same underlying value proposition logic (transformation + who it's for), three different tone treatments:

---

### FRIENDLY (e.g. salon / cafe tone)
**Headline:** Speak up without the awkward pause.
**Sub-headline:** For anyone who's ever blanked mid-sentence — we'll help you find the words, every single time.

### PROFESSIONAL (e.g. clinic / agency tone)
**Headline:** Develop fluent, confident spoken English.
**Sub-headline:** Designed for students and professionals who require measurable improvement in verbal communication, taught with bilingual instruction where needed.

### CONFIDENT-SIMPLE (Vivekananda Spoken English Academy's actual tone)
**Headline:** Speak confidently in English — starting with your first class.
**Sub-headline:** For students, job-seekers, and Hindi-speaking learners in T. Nagar who know English on paper but freeze up the moment they have to speak it.

---

**Why this matters for the repo:** The Master System Prompt + Business Brief structure didn't change. Only the tone instruction changed, and the headline/sub-headline pair shifted appropriately while keeping the same core value proposition. This is what makes the system sellable to multiple clients, not just usable once.
