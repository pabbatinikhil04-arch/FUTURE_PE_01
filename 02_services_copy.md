**Who it's for:** School and college students, and beginners, who understand English but hesitate to speak it in class or in public.

**What's included:**
- Step-by-step grammar and vocabulary building, taught with Hindi-to-English bridging for first-time English speakers

**Why choose this program here:** Because trainers teach in both Hindi and English, you're never stuck not understanding the lesson itself — you learn English in a language you already speak.
